WHY TOPOLOGY MATTERS

A mesh with hidden defects will silently break your pipeline — from sculpting to rigging to final render. TopolGuard finds them before your tools do.

01

DEFORMATION

Bad topology warps under rigging. Hidden non-manifold edges create unpredictable deformation artifacts in character animation.

02

PIPELINE

Non-manifold geometry breaks boolean operations, UV unwrapping, and 3D printing slicers. One bad edge cascades through every downstream tool.

03

RENDER

Flipped normals and degenerate faces cause shading artifacts, invisible geometry, and wasted render cycles in real-time engines.

[02]

WHAT WE CHECK

01

NON-MANIFOLD EDGES

Edges shared by 0 or 3+ faces. Break booleans, 3D print slicers, and UV unwrapping.

02

SKINNY TRIANGLES

Near-zero area or extreme aspect ratios. Cause shading artifacts and simulation instability.

03

N-GONS

Faces with 5 or more vertices. Tessellate unpredictably across renderers and game engines.

04

FLIPPED NORMALS

Faces pointing inward instead of outward. Render invisible or inverted in real-time engines.
